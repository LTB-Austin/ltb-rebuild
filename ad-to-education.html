FAQ

Ad-to-Education Sequence™ — common questions

What is an Ad-to-Education Sequence™?

A sequenced content system: a short ad earns the click, then a longer education video earns the belief — moving shoppers from awareness to conversion, in order.

Where does an Ad-to-Education Sequence™ run?

Anywhere your product sells — Amazon, Walmart, Chewy, Target, retailer media networks, and your own product pages.

What's in the sequence?

Typically a 15-second video ad built for paid placement, followed by a 30-second education video on the product page that delivers the Science Story™.

Does it actually move sales?

Yes. For Designs for Health Vitamin D, the Ad-to-Education Sequence™ drove +45% CTR and +42% ROAS on Amazon.

How is it different from a regular video ad?

A single ad has to explain everything at once. A sequence splits the job — awareness first, education second — so each piece performs.

Stop explaining everything in one ad.

Sequence it instead: awareness first, education second, conversion as the result.
