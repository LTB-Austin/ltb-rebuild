Our Story

Since 2013, Let There Be has helped healthcare brands turn complex science into clear, engaging stories — first as a world-class studio for MOA videos and science animation, trusted by teams across Bayer, Haleon, Kenvue, Opella, Karo Healthcare, Nestlé Health Science, Dr. Reddy's, and the industry's own CHPA.

Over a decade of that work taught us something: the brands that win aren't the ones with the best creative. They're the ones whose creative is built from their science. So we built the Science Marketing Engine around three moves we live by — prove it, persuade with it, produce it — powered by Science Intelligence, Science Story, and Science Studio.
