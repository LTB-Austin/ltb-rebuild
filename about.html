About us.

Your science is remarkable.
Our job is to make sure everyone knows it.

You've done the hard part. We make sure the people who need to hear about it actually do — not buried in jargon, not lost in a PDF, but out in the world, doing what it was always meant to do.

Our Story

Since 2013, Let There Be has helped healthcare brands turn complex science into clear, engaging stories — first as a world-class studio for MOA videos and science animation, trusted by teams across Bayer, Haleon, Kenvue, Opella, Karo Healthcare, Nestlé Health Science, Dr. Reddy's, and the industry's own CHPA.

Over a decade of that work taught us something: the brands that win aren't the ones with the best creative. They're the ones whose creative is built from their science. So we built the Science Marketing Engine — an operating model where science leads the strategy, creativity brings it to life, and production scales it everywhere.

How the Engine Is Organized

Science leads.

Lit Review™, Claim Dev™, Science Story™, and science education.

Creativity amplifies.

Concepts, copy, visuals, and MOA Moments™ built from the science.

Accounts connect.

One team owning client experience across science, creative, and production.

The Science Studio scales.

Flexible execution through our studio network and trusted partners.
