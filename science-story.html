Science Marketing Engine — Phase 03

Science Story™

Most brands treat clinical data like legal protection. We treat it like fuel.

We transform your science into creative concepts — new claims, new positioning, new reasons to believe.

Science Story™ is our proprietary framework for turning clinical data, real-world evidence, and category knowledge into a narrative that unlocks new claims, new positioning, and new reasons for consumers to believe. The product of more over a decade in consumer health.

Instead of asking:

"What can we say?"

We ask:

"What have we not discovered yet?"

What Makes It Different
  • We start with the real regulatory constraints — not a fantasy playing field.
  • We don't just read your science — we interrogate it.
  • We look beyond the obvious claim, using a structured curiosity framework.
  • Human + AI, in that order — our team first, AI as an accelerator inside our framework.
  • We score ideas like investors, not copywriters — defensibility and strategic value, not beauty.
The Process

  1. Expert conversations

    Legal, regulatory, and medical experts define the real playing field early.

  2. Digging for Gems™

    Curated, evidence-backed insights — each one factual, substantiable, specific, and strategically directed.

  3. Science Story™ development & Polishing Gems™

    We present coherent strategic directions — not a buffet of loose facts — then refine the chosen one together with your team: claims, narrative, and substantiation.

  4. Consumer testing

    Healthcare brands expect ideas to be validated — so we test for resonance, clarity, and believability with consumers or HCPs. We can run testing through our partners, or package everything cleanly for the testing partners you already trust.

  5. Activation Planning Session

    Once the Science Story™ is delivered, our team sits down with yours to onboard it — and to brainstorm how to put it to work across your channels. We don't hand you a strategy and walk away; we help you activate it.

FAQ

Science Story™ — common questions

What is a Science Story™?

Your evidence, turned into ownable positioning and a narrative competitors can't easily copy. It's the full output of the Science Marketing Engine.

What's included in a Science Story™?

A literature review, a claims architecture, and the polished narrative — ownable positioning, audience messaging, and creative direction, ready to activate across channels.

How is a Science Story™ different from a brand story?

A brand story is built from creative instinct. A Science Story™ is built from your science first, so it's both compelling and defensible under scrutiny.

How long does a Science Story™ take?

Weeks, not months — and it becomes the foundation every downstream video, ad, and campaign is built from.

What can I do with a Science Story™?

Activate it everywhere: MOA Moment™ videos, social, HCP education, retail and PDP content, influencer kits, and ad-to-education sequences.

What's your Science Story™?

Every consumer health brand has a compelling Science Story™ waiting to be told.
