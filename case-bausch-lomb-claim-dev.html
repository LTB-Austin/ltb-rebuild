Proof — Case Study

Bausch + Lomb
83 studies. 35 new claims. Three weeks.

Science Intelligence™

A new product for an underserved consumer health need — and a launch team that needed to know exactly what could be said about it, compliantly, before committing to the formula.

The Story

Bausch + Lomb had gathered a strong, well-organized body of clinical literature across the formulation's ingredients — what it didn't yet have was a claims layer: that evidence graded and tied directly to compliant claim language. The requirement: structure-function claims that trace directly back to source studies and survive medical, legal, and regulatory review. The audience: healthcare professionals — skeptical readers who check the references.

Our Claim Dev™ engine organized the full evidence base into a graded, searchable claims library, developed compliant structure-function claims tied to source studies, and re-verified every claim-supporting study against the published record — authors, N, endpoints.

The claims were approved by Bausch + Lomb's medical, legal, and regulatory teams in three weeks — informed by two specialist advisory boards. A complete, MLR-approved claims roadmap, delivered while the product was still being formulated.
