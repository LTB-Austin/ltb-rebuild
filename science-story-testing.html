Science Story™ — Consumer Testing

We test the story before you spend on it.

Every Science Story™ can be put in front of real consumers first — so you back the concept and the claims that actually move people, before a dollar goes to production.

Our consumer-research partner
What We Test

Each story becomes a concept, in three layers.

The headline

The single line that leads the concept — the hook a consumer sees first.

The narrative

The short story that frames the idea and gives the headline its meaning.

The claim cluster

A group of specific, evidence-backed claims that live under the concept.

We build two to three complete concepts, then test them head-to-head so the direction you take to market is the one consumers actually chose.

See It Through Their Eyes

Consumers highlight the words that land.

In a highlighter heat map, people mark what draws them in and what pushes them away — so we see, line by line, exactly what’s working in the copy.
