Science Story™ — Consumer Testing

We test the story before you spend on it.

Every Science Story™ can be put in front of real consumers first — so you back the concept and the claims that actually move people, before a dollar goes to production.

What We Test

Each story becomes a concept, in three layers.

The headline

The single line that leads the concept — the hook a consumer sees first.

The narrative

The short story that frames the idea and gives the headline its meaning.

The claim cluster

A group of specific, evidence-backed claims that live under the concept.

We build two to three complete concepts, then test them head-to-head so the direction you take to market is the one consumers actually chose.

What We Measure

Reactions, layer by layer.

Overall
  • Purchase intent — would it move them to buy
  • Uniqueness — does it stand apart
  • Believability — do they trust it
The headline
  • Appeal
  • Whether it grabs attention
The narrative
  • Appeal
  • Believability
The claims
  • Believability
  • Motivation to purchase
How We Read It

Signal you can act on.

Like / dislike heat maps

Consumers highlight the exact words that land — and the ones that don’t — so we can see, line by line, what’s working in the copy.

Claim optimization score

Believability and purchase motivation combine into a single score per claim, showing which combination reaches the most consumers.

Claim ranking (MaxDiff)

A max-difference exercise ranks every claim by its power to drive purchase — a clear, relative read of what to lead with.

We also look at which audiences find the story most appealing, so positioning and targeting are grounded in evidence from the start.

What You Walk Away With
  • The winning concept — the headline and narrative consumers preferred
  • A prioritized set of claims, ranked by what actually persuades
  • A read on the audiences most drawn to the story
  • Confidence to produce, knowing the direction is validated — not a guess

Testing runs quickly and quantitatively with a dedicated consumer-research partner, and folds right into the Science Story™ process.
