FAQ

Claim Dev™ — common questions

What is Claim Dev™?

Claim Dev™ turns your science into a hierarchy of marketing claims that are substantiated, defensible, and approvable — each tied directly to the source studies that support it.

What does Claim Dev™ deliver?

A graded, searchable claims library: structure-function claims mapped to evidence, source-log verification, and language built to survive medical, legal, and regulatory (MLR) review.

Will the claims survive MLR?

That's the point. Every claim is evidence-graded and traceable to the published record, so your legal and regulatory teams can verify it quickly and approve with confidence.

How long does Claim Dev™ take?

Typically a few weeks. In one engagement, a 35-claim, MLR-approved roadmap was delivered in three weeks — while the product was still being formulated.

Do I need a Lit Review™ first?

Often, yes. Lit Review™ surfaces the evidence; Claim Dev™ organizes it into approvable claims. Together they form the foundation of your Science Story™.

Have science but no claims architecture?

Claim Dev™ turns your existing studies into marketing language that survives scrutiny.
