Initiatives

Science is how we earn a living.
Generosity is how we want to be remembered.

We've built homes in the Philippines following Typhoon Haiyan, filmed fundraising videos to help orphans in India, and traveled to Mozambique to document a cashew brand bringing hope to impoverished communities.

The same skills we use to tell our clients' Science Stories™ — research, storytelling, film, and design — are the ones we've lent to causes that need them most. When our team can use what we're good at to do real good, we show up.

Where Our Hearts Are

EVAC

When the U.S. withdrew from Afghanistan — leaving behind tens of thousands of Afghan Allies to be hunted by the Taliban — members of our team helped fund and launch EVAC: a volunteer-run 501(c)(3) non-profit that protects, evacuates, and resettles at-risk Afghans who stood with America.

Let There Be has been a proud supporter of EVAC's mission, and many of our team have been deeply involved in its life-saving work — helping families stay safe and healthy as they hide from the Taliban and make their way to freedom.

How EVAC Helps

Protect.

Safe houses, digital-security kits, and intel keep families hidden from the Taliban.

Evacuate.

Immigration filings, travel documents, and flights move families toward freedom.

Resettle.

Food, medical care, and resettlement services help families rebuild their lives.
