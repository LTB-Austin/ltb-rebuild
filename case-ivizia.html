The Story

As lead agency for the 2022 launch of iVIZIA, we had to introduce a new dry-eye brand and establish credibility against major competitors with far greater awareness.

We developed a Science Story™ called 'We see a better way,' centered on iVIZIA's multi-moisturizing formulation — three unique ingredients working together against eye dryness. The launch campaign paired engaging visuals with clear messaging, and video assets explained the formulation's benefits across channels.
