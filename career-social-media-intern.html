Careers — Open Role

Social Media Intern

Internship · Hybrid / Flex · Remote (North & South America only)

We’re looking for a motivated Social Media Intern to take full ownership of the Let There Be LinkedIn presence — from ideation through to posting.

Overview

About the role

Let There Be is a science marketing agency that helps leading healthcare brands communicate and visualize science in clear, ownable, regulatory-approved ways. Our work lives at the intersection of science and creativity, and we’re growing our presence to match the quality of what we create.

This is not a supporting role. You’ll be responsible for the entire content pipeline, from ideation through to posting. If you think in content, move fast, and want real ownership over a brand channel from day one, this is that opportunity.

It’s an ideal launchpad for anyone considering a career in marketing: an inside look at how a working agency operates, a real portfolio of published work, and hands-on experience across strategy, content creation, and execution.

Compensation & Structure

The details

  • Compensation — weekly stipend; amount confirmed upon offer.
  • Time commitment — flexible and part-time, estimated 8–12 hours per week. Flexible scheduling for candidates in school (part-time during the semester, full-time on breaks).
  • Duration — internship-based, with potential to extend based on performance and fit.
  • Location — fully remote (North & South America only). Availability during Eastern Time business hours for check-ins and approvals is expected.
Core Responsibilities

What you’ll do

  • Content ideation — generate a consistent pipeline of post ideas that reflect our voice, expertise, and work, from thought leadership to behind-the-scenes.
  • Copywriting — write clear, on-brand captions and post copy for LinkedIn, tailored to healthcare brand marketers and industry partners.
  • Visual creation — produce or source supporting visuals within our brand guidelines.
  • Approval workflow — submit content for internal review and incorporate feedback before scheduling.
  • Posting & scheduling — manage the calendar and publish consistently on the LTB LinkedIn page.
  • Performance awareness — track basic engagement metrics and flag what’s working to inform future content.
  • Process building — develop and document a repeatable content system: calendar framework, approval workflow, and posting cadence.
What We’re Looking For

You might be a fit if…

  • A genuine interest in marketing, content creation, or social media — ideally with hands-on experience managing a brand or personal account.
  • Strong writing skills with an instinct for voice, tone, and audience.
  • Comfort creating simple graphics or short-form visual content (Canva, Adobe Express, or similar).
  • Highly organized and self-directed — you can run a content calendar without being micromanaged.
  • An interest in science, healthcare, or B2B marketing is a plus, but curiosity and willingness to learn the space matter more.
  • Someone who takes initiative and brings ideas to the table unprompted.
How We’ll Start

The process

If your background aligns, we’ll invite you to complete a short creative challenge — a creative writing assignment, a 60-second video introduction, or a sample LinkedIn post. It’s intentional: we want to work with people excited enough to show us what they can do.

Strong candidates will be invited to a brief introductory call, and from there we’ll move quickly toward an agreed start date.
