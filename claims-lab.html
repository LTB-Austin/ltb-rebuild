Claims Lab™

The bridge between R&D and marketing.

Keep exploration continuously open — between the teams, every month.

What It Is

Claims Lab™ is an ongoing retainer that sits between your R&D and marketing teams and keeps scientific, claim, and story exploration continuously open. Either team can bring an idea forward — and Let There Be proactively generates them too.

Who it's for: brands with active science and an ambitious marketing team — where the next outsized advantage is hiding in a thread no one has had time to chase. It's an offshoot of Claim Dev™: same scientific rigor, run continuously instead of project-by-project.

The Problem

R&D and claim teams never have time to chase every thread.

Yet any one of those threads could become an outsized advantage. Claims Lab™ breaks the bottleneck — a standing space where ideas get explored before the moment passes.

How It Works

Ideas come from three places.

Marketing

The brand team spots something in the news, the competition, or consumer behavior.

R&D

The science team sees a new mechanism, ingredient, or possible benefit worth pursuing.

Let There Be

We generate and explore a set number of fresh ideas every month — proactively.

An Example

A brand-team member spots a competing ingredient making headlines. A thread opens — and the lab runs it down:

1

Map the field

Find the competitive claims already in market.

2

Review the science

Comb the published literature for what's known.

3

Establish the evidence

Pin down exactly what the data supports.

4

Test what's allowable

Explore which claims could survive MLR and regulatory review.

5

Draft the Science Story™

Shape a potential narrative around the strongest angle.

6

Decide

Is the thread an outsized advantage — or one to set aside? Either answer is a win.

Why It's Different

Claim Dev™ organizes the claims you already know you need. Claims Lab™ is the standing engine that keeps finding the ones you don't yet know to look for.

It's continuous, not a one-off — a monthly cadence of exploration that compounds. The cost of missing an outsized advantage is far higher than the cost of keeping the lab open.

FAQ

Claims Lab™ — common questions

What is Claims Lab™?

An ongoing retainer that sits between your R&D and marketing teams and keeps scientific, claim, and story exploration continuously open. Either team can bring ideas forward, and Let There Be proactively generates and explores a set number every month.

How is it different from Claim Dev™?

Claim Dev™ is a project that organizes a defined set of claims from your existing science. Claims Lab™ is the standing, monthly version of that rigor — an offshoot that keeps hunting for advantages you don't yet know to look for.

Where do the ideas come from?

Three places: your marketing team (news, competition, consumer behavior), your R&D team (new mechanisms, ingredients, or benefits), and Let There Be (a set number of proactively generated ideas each month).

What happens to an idea once it enters the lab?

We map the competitive claims, review the literature, establish what the evidence supports, explore what's allowable, draft a potential Science Story™, and decide whether the thread is an outsized advantage worth pursuing.

Do we need to be running the full Science Marketing Engine?

No. Claims Lab™ can run alongside the Engine or as a standalone retainer for teams that already have active science and want a continuous source of claim and story exploration.

Want a standing lab between your science and your story?

Claims Lab™ keeps the threads open — so the next outsized advantage doesn't slip by.
