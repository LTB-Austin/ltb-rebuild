FAQ

Claims Lab™ — common questions

What is Claims Lab™?

A flexible, premium retainer for research, claims, and narratives. It runs the same sequence (territories, research, MLR approval, Science Story™, validation) as a standing capability — so you always have go-ready claims on hand and never miss the next great one.

How does it run — and for how long?

However your category demands. Run it always-on, month to month, to stay ahead all year — or as a focused push that loads up meaningfully new Science Stories™ before a planning cycle. It's a retainer, so you flex the cadence and scope with us.

How is it different from Claim Dev™?

Claim Dev™ is a project that organizes a defined set of claims from your existing science. Claims Lab™ runs that same rigor as an ongoing capability — continually imagining new territories, researching them, clearing MLR, shaping Science Stories™, and validating the strongest with consumers.

Do you test the claims and stories?

Yes. The strongest Science Stories™ are validated with real consumers before you commit — so you build on what actually resonates, not on a guess.

Do we need the full Science Marketing Engine?

No. Claims Lab™ can run alongside the Engine or on its own, for teams with active science that want an ongoing capability for claim and story development.
