The Work
The MOA
The Story

Qunol asked us to distinguish Advanced Omega in the crowded Omega-3 segment, where consumers believe all products offer similar benefits — unaware that absorption differences dramatically change results.

After reviewing the bioavailability research, we developed new positioning with full brand, legal, and regulatory approval: many fish oil supplements are difficult to absorb; Qunol Advanced Omega delivers 10x better absorption than traditional fish oils. It activated in a national TV campaign featuring a doctor and an MOA animation.
