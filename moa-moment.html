FAQ

MOA Moment™ — common questions

What is an MOA Moment™?

An MOA Moment™ is a short, vivid visualization of your product's mechanism of action — the science explained in seconds: clear, compelling, and compliant.

Where are MOA Moments™ used?

Social, retail and PDP, HCP education, influencer content, and TV and digital ads — anywhere you need to make the science instantly understandable.

How long is an MOA Moment™?

Built for modern attention spans — typically seconds, and designed to land with or without sound on mobile-first feeds.

Are MOA Moments™ compliant?

Yes. They're built from a substantiated claims foundation, so the science shown holds up to medical, legal, and regulatory review.

What makes a good MOA Moment™?

Real mechanism science, simplified into a visual story people can picture — because if people can picture it, they can believe it.

What does your product actually do?

If consumers can't picture the mechanism, they can't believe the claim. Let's fix that in ten seconds.
